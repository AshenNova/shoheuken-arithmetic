Set up

Meeting ID: 6596626547<-- Key in or just click on the link

General idea:

Student would need 2 to 3 devices each to connected to Zoom.

All devices will be logged into the same meeting (No account creation is needed)

Please setup at a location with good internet connection and relatively conducive environment

  1. Device A:
    2. Acting as a visualizer
    • Device A would preferably be a phone/ipad looking down on their work (View photo below).
      Placed device A on a stable structure. (Stack of books, files, tissue box etc)
      Table has to be sufficiently large to accomodate everything.
    • Device A's screen will always be left on hence ensure that sufficient battery or charger is available
      • Set screen timeout to "never" or "none"
      • Set screen brightness to the lowest setting
    • The above example is about 23cm tall.
      • Place an exercise book underneath and adjust the height of the camera. The height may differ on quality of the camera.

    • Put the phone horizontally (Landscape) to change its orientation before leaving it on the platform.

      If the view does not rotate, check that the rotate lock is removed

      Please also use the back camera (Screen facing upwards) (Usually better quality)

    • May consider putting a heavy object on the other end of the phone to make it stick out even more.
      • Should be able to at least see:
    • Table lamp might be necessary to brighten up the place and get rid of some shadows.

  2. Device B:
    3. Desktop/Laptop + earpiece/headphone with a microphone: Will be used for viewing work and communicating

    1. Open Shoheuken-arithmetic
    2. Join Zoom meeting ID: 6596626547<-- Key in or just click on the link
    3. Wait to be accepted
    4. Sharescreen
    5. Turn on camera when ready.
  3. Device C (Optional):
    4. Device C facing the screen of Device B so we know what is on the screen

Lower the volume of Device A and C to none, otherwise there would be echoes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Question: Do i need sim card for Zoom?
Answer: No, internet will do.
You do not need to download the app (unless you want to), just click on the link will do.
Question: Can i use other devices as Device B such as a tablet?
Answer: Yes, but instead of sharing "screen", you have to share via "website url" so that i can have the annotate function on my end
Any device less than 11 inch would probably be excruciating to use.
